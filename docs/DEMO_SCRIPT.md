## Product Explanation

Similia AI is an AI Clinical Workspace for Classical Homeopaths.

It helps a practitioner move from raw symptoms to structured case-taking, missing questions, rubric selection, repertorization, materia medica comparison, doctor-approved prescription, fee record, printable documents, and patient timeline.

AI does not prescribe automatically. The final prescription, potency, repetition, and clinical decision are made by the practitioner.
